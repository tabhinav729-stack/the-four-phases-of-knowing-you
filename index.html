I once told her,
“Rome wasn’t built in a day.”
She smiled —
and quietly built it anyway.
Now, as she leaves,
Rome stands ready —
made of calm, laughter, and light.
This space holds five poems for her —
a beginning, a becoming, a bond,
Nothing to decode here —
just moments that meant something,
arranged gently in their own time.
for Payal.

Naram Par Atoot

Woh kehti hai, “Main thodi weird hoon.” Shayad isiliye vo samajh nahi aati, par mehsoos ho jaati hai. Usme kuch alag hai — shaant, par mazboot; jaise leher bhi ruk jaaye uski soch ke aage. Uske andaaz mein halki si masti hai, aur nazron mein ek gehra samandar — badi bhuri gehri aankhein, jo muskaan ke saath sunehri chamak uthaati hain, jaise dil mei savera hogaya ho. Uski hansi mein halki si khanak hai — jaise roshni ne ghungroo baandh liye ho. Kabhi kuch kehna bhi zaroori nahi lagta, bas uske hone se sannata bhi zinda lagne lagta hai. Kabhi kabhi voh bhi thak jaati hai, par uski roshni kabhi nahi rukta — woh andheron ko bhi ujala dene ka junoon rakhti hai, chahe uske khud ke haath hi kyu na jal jaayein. Par uski thakaan bhi ek roshni thi Taqat agar insaan hoti, to woh uske jaisi hoti — naram bhi, atoot bhi. Usne sikhaya, ki taqat ko shor nahi chaahiye; bas apne aaspaas sab kuch santulit kar dene ki himmat chaahiye. Kabhi kabhi main bhi uski khamoshi mein baith kar samajhta hoon, shor kam karne ke liye awaaz nahi, bas niyat badalni padti hai. Aur main sochta hoon, kya kabhi main bhi aisa ho paunga — shaant, par mazboot, bilkul uske jaisa. Woh nahi ghabraati thi — na kal se, na kismet se. Shayad use pata tha, kuch taaqatein humse nahi, humare saath chalti hain. Uska vishwas aisa tha, jaise har raaste ke mod par koi roshni use dekh kar muskurati ho. Woh hi to hai, ek ujala jo jalaata nahi, par aaspaas sab kuch roshan kar deta hai.
“Some lights don’t burn — they just brighten everything around them.”

Thehraav Mein Taqat

Shor se bhara tha din, aur phir voh aayi — jaise thak chuki hawaon ko kisi ne halka sa sahla diya ho. Uske hone se afra-tafri bhi sambhal jaati thi, log dheere bolne lagte, aur jazbaat soch ban jaate. Jaise uski nazar ke aas-paas duniya ne thoda sa santulan seekh liya ho. Kabhi keh kar samjhaati thi, kabhi sirf dekhti rehti — uske lafzon mein nami thi, aur moun mein gehraai. Jab main uljhanon mein bhatakta tha, voh kehti, “Jo badal sakta hai, us par kaam karo, jo nahi, us se jeena seekho.” Aur tab lagta — zindagi utni kathin nahi jitni main soch leta hoon. Usne sikhaya, taqat awaaz nahi hoti — woh ek ehsaas hoti hai, jahan sirf uske hone se sab kuch sambhal jaata hai. Kabhi uske saath baith kar samjha, thehraav bolta nahi — bas behta hai. Uske saath hone par lagta tha, jaise saans bhi apni raftaar bhool jaati ho. Ab jab zindagi tez bhaagti hai, main us jaise thehraav dhoondhta hoon — jo shabd se nahi, ek muskaan se raah mil jaaye.
“In her stillness, life learned balance.”

Sahaj Aur Sacchi

Shayad har kahani mein ek aisa insaan hota hai, jo shor nahi karta, par sab mehsoos karta hai. Jo saath dikhata nahi, par kabhi chhodta bhi nahi. Woh aisi hi thi — sahaj bhi, aur sacchi bhi. Har mushkil mein, bina bulaye aa jaati thi, jaise sambhalna uski aadat ho, farz nahi. Uske hone se duniya ka shor thoda thahar jaata tha, par iss baar, uski chup mein sukoon nahi, ek samajh thi. Kabhi hans ke rokti thi, kabhi daant ke samjha deti thi — aur jab sab theek ho jaata, toh hasti hue keh deti, “ab mila na ‘primary data’?” jaise mazaak ke andar hi sabak chhupa ho. Dosti uske liye farz nahi thi, woh to ek rishta tha jo dil se nibhaaya gaya. Jab log andaazon se faisle banate the, woh unke beech nishabd khadi rehti — nazar mein woh yakeen hota, jo lafzon se zyada sambhal leta tha. Un dino duniya poochti thi, “tum dono ke beech aisa kya hai?” Aur mujhe samajhne mein waqt lag gaya — ki kuch rishte jazbaat nahi, ek dua ki tarah jeeye jaate hain. Kabhi uske saath baith kar mehsoos hua, ki kuch log zindagi mein “kab tak” ke liye nahi, “kaise” ke liye milte hain — kaise behtar banna hai, kaise sambhalna hai. Tab samjha, uski khamoshi bhi ek bojh thi, har sach uske andar ghoomta rehta tha, aur duniya use sirf thehraav samajh baithi thi. Aur tab samjha — sach aur sahaj hona alag cheez nahi, woh to ek hi prakash ke do rang hain; ek dil ko roshan karta hai, aur ek zindagi ko.
“Sometimes a person isn’t just a friend — they’re the quietest, truest answer to your life.”

Pehla Pyaar

Pehle toh laga, pyaar sirf kahaniyon ka jadoo hota hai — jahan sab kuch likha hua lagta hai, par mehsoos kabhi nahi hota. Phir voh mili — aur har kissa asli ban gaya. Uske saath sab kuch halkasa ho jaata — din ka shor bhi, raat ka bojh bhi. Woh baat karti, toh duniya thodi dheemi ho jaati, jaise waqt uski awaaz ka intezaar kar raha ho. Uske haath pakadne mein ek ajeeb sa sukoon tha — na koi drama, na koi daawa, bas woh, main, aur beech mein ek sannata, jo dono ke dilon ke liye kafi tha. Kabhi voh zid karti, bachchon jaisi masoomi se kehti — “please please please!” Aur main hamesha maan jaata, kyunki uski har zid mein mohabbat chhupi hoti thi. Uske pyaar mein main mehfooz bhi tha, aur naya bhi. Har roz kuch aur seekhta — khud ke baare mein, aur uske dil ke andar chhupe sansaar ke baare mein. Kabhi kabhi woh bhi thak jaati thi — apne lafzon ke beech khud se ladti thi, aankhon mein thoda sa dard, par chalne ka jazba usse zyada gehra. Usne kabhi kisi “knight in shining armour” ka intezaar nahi kiya, bas apne zakhmon pe ujala rakhna seekh liya. Phir bhi, jab main uske paas chupchaap khada tha, usne mujhe apni shaanti ke daayre mein sameta. Aur main aaj bhi shukriya karta hoon, ki usne apni taqat ke beech mere hone ki jagah banayi. Uske saath hone ka matlab tha bas ek baat samajhna — ke pyaar sirf sundar logon ka nahi hota, pyaar toh unka hota hai jo toot kar bhi kisi ko poora kar dete hain. Ab jab bhi hawa chalti hai, lagta hai uske haath meri gardan ke paas phir ghoom gaye hain, ussi nazakat se, jaise kehti ho — “Aap safe ho, Buddhu.” Aur tab samajh aata hai — pyaar woh nahi jo zindagi badalta hai, pyaar toh woh hai, jisme aap poore ban jaate ho.
“Love doesn’t complete you; it reminds you that you were whole all along.”

Yaar Payal

Yaar Payal, Aap soch rahe hoge — main already chaar poem likh chuka hoon aapke baare mein, ab aur kya baaki reh gaya hai? Par aap nahi jaanti, aapke baare mein kehne ke liye mere paas alfaazon se zyada ehsaas hain. Kisi ke liye aap chulbuli shaitaan ho, kisi ke liye sahej samjhi hui. Kisi ko aapki bebak baatein atpati lagti hain, toh kisi ko kuch zyada hi chatpati. Par yaar aapki kasam, mujhe aapki har baat achchi lagti hai. Sachmei thode weird toh ho aap — hasti ho toh duniya bhool jaati hai aur sabko hasa deti ho, roti ho toh akele mein ghut jaati ho. Kabhi bina baat ke bahut khush ho jaati ho, toh kabhi bina warning shant ho jaati ho. Aap ek raaz ho — khulte nahi kisi ke saamne. Dikhti sabko ho, par nazar kisi ko aati nahi. Tod dena chahti ho khud ko kisi mein, par bharosa karne se pehle dil hamesha rok leta hai. Mujhe shayad aapke baare mein sab kuch nahi pata, par pata nahi kyun, aapke baare mein sab kuch pata lagta hai. Aapki aankhein bahut kuch kehti hain, par alfaaz tak baatein aane nahi deti. Ghabrati ho — agar duniya sunn legi inhe, toh shayad aapko samjhegi nahi. Mujhe pata hai, duniya abhi thodi na-insaaf lag rahi hai — aur roz ek 23 saal ka buddhu bhi aake pareshan kar raha hai. Sab kuch ek saath badal raha hai, aur aap bas saans le kar sambhalne ki koshish kar rahi ho. Par yakeen maano, sab theek ho jaata hai — hamesha hota hai. Aapne pehle bhi sabse andheri raaton ko dekha hai, aur phir bhi roshni ban kar nikli ho. Yeh waqt bhi bas ek mod hai, ek rukawat nahi. Payal, aap ladne wali nahi — jeetne wali ho. Kabhi kabhi, jab dil thak jaata hai sab kuch samjhane se, toh khamoshi khud ek prarthana ban jaati hai. Us waqt bas apne aap ko bhagwan ke hawaale kar dena — woh hi to hai jo toofan ke beech kishti ko kinare tak laata hai. Aur jab ye toofan halka sa tehar jaaye aur phir door se zameen dikhe, toh shayad aap mujhe wahin dekhengi, kinare par, haath hilaate hue, intezaar mein, poori shiddat se muskurata hua, do cup chai ke sath. I really hope, the faith you brought to me, brings you back to me. Kyunki — hum toh teh hi Lodha ke Anuskha aur Kohli, Mulank 1 aur 5, Leo aur Aries, ek ajeeb si jodi, par bilkul apni kismat ke hisaab se likhi hui. Aur haan — aasan nahi hai aapse pyaar karna, par aapko pyaar na karna, uss se bhi zyaada mushkil hai.
“Some stories never end; they just wait for the right time to continue.”

If you ever lose your calm,
remember — you are someone’s peace.

- Abhinav
