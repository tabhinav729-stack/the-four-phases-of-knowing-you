This space holds five poems.

Each one belongs to a phase — a beginning, a becoming, a bond, a memory, and a moment that stayed.
Nothing to decode here.
Just moments that meant something, arranged gently in their own time.
Start wherever you wish.
You’ll find a bit of us in each.
for Payal.

Naram Par Atoot

Woh kehti hai, “Main thodi weird hoon.” Shayad isiliye samajh nahi aati, bas mehsoos hoti hoon. Woh sabse alag hai — shaant, par mazboot. Uske andaaz mein masti hai, aur soch mein gehraai. Usme ek ajeeb sa sukoon hai, jaise zindagi ne use thoda zyada samjha ho. Na shor, na dikhawa, sirf ek noor, jo sab keh jaata hai. Taqat agar insaan hoti, to woh Payal jaise hoti — narm, par atoot; bal bhi, aur shanti bhi. Usne sikhaya, ki taqat ko ghamand se nahi, sahajta se jeena chahiye. Woh hi to hai, ek roshni jo jalaati nahi, par aaspaas sab kuch roshan kar deti hai.
“Aur shayad isi liye, uske hone se duniya thodi behtar lagti hai.”

Thehraav Mein Taqat

Kuch log shor mein bhi thehraav laate hain, woh unmein se ek thi. Na kuch zyada, na kuch kam, bas jitni zarurat thi duniya ko sambhalne ki. Uske har lafz mein ek narmi thi, jo gusse ko bhi soch mein badal deti. Jaise har mushkil ke andar ek halki si roshni chhupa di ho usne. Woh sikhati thi bina shabd ke, ki taqat awaaz se nahi, santulan se dikhai jaati hai, aur izzat, sirf bartav se milti hai. Uske andaaz mein ek sukoon tha, na dikhawa, na dabav. Bas ek halka sa noor, jo sab kuch aasaan bana deta tha. Kabhi kabhi sochta hoon, kaise itni shaanti ek insaan mein sama sakti hai. Jaise zindagi ne use thoda zyada samjha diya ho baaki sabse. Aur pata nahi kaise, par uski wo shaant si seekh meri zindagi ka hissa ban gayi. Ab har afra-tafri mein, main usi jaisa thehraav dhoondhta hoon.
“Zindagi ke daud mein, usne thehraav likh diya.”

Sahaj Aur Sacchi

Kuch log shor nahi karte, bas saath ban jaate hain zindagi ka. Woh aisi hi thi — bina kahe sab mehsoos kara jaane wali. Har mushkil mein, bina bulaye aa jaati thi, jaise zimmedaari nahi, aadat ho sambhalne ki. Uske hone se duniya ka shor thoda shaant ho jaata tha. Uska saath koi likha hua wada nahi tha, par har pal nibhaya gaya jaise dua nibhaayi jaati ho. Bina shart, bina awaaz, sirf ek sachcha ehsaas tha. Kabhi hans ke rokti thi, kabhi daant ke samjha deti thi — jaise sambhalna uske jeene ka tarika tha. Dosti uske liye farz nahi thi, woh to ek rishta tha jo dil se nibhaaya gaya. Aur jab log samajhne se pehle hi faisla suna dete the, woh un sab ke beech khadi rahi — mujhe samajhne ke liye, mujhe todne ke bajaye, jodne ke liye. Kya kehte hain aise logon ko? Jo dost se badhkar, dua lagte hain — jo apne hone se hi tumhe behtar bana dete hain.
“Kabhi kabhi ek insaan sirf dost nahi hota, poori zindagi ka sabse khubsoorat jawab hota hai.”

Sukoon Bhari Yaad

Usse milna, jaise pehli dafa dil ka darwaza khula ho, zameen se judi, par sapno jaisi. Jo raat ke teen baje bhi uth ke keh deti thi, “Safe flight, I love you,” aur jab phone chori ho gaya tha, toh bina kahe naya laa di thi. Woh sirf pyaar nahi karti thi, woh pyaar ko jeeti thi — Teej waale din tithi ke hisaab se wish karne par bhavuk ho jaane wali, aur Shivling ko dil se apnane wali, jaise har dua uske haath se nikal kar, seedha mujhe milti ho. Woh apni duniya khud sambhalne wali thi, apni ladai khud ladti thi. Phir bhi, pata nahi kaise, mujhe apni kahani ka chhota sa hissa bana liya, aur bas wahi, mere liye sab kuch tha. Main kabhi uska savior banna nahi chahta tha, sirf itna chahta tha, ki jab duniya uljhe, main uske paas khada rahoon, tab tak, jab tak woh khud raasta dhoondh le. Train rides, vada anna waale breaks, bowling nights, pasta, Vasu ka sandwich — sab yaadon ke thaal mein ab bhi garam hain. Vo papa ke phone se chupke message karti thi, bhale bimar ho, taaki uska buddhu pareshan na ho. Usne kabhi kaha tha, “Jo aapne do mahine mein mehsoos karaya, vo saalon mein koi nahi kar saka.” Aur main aaj bhi shukriya kehna chahta hoon, ke usne mujhe apne bharose ke laayak samjha.
“Kuch kahaniyaan khatam nahi hoti, sirf thoda ruk jaati hain sahi waqt ka intezaar karte hue.”

Us Pal Ka Sukoon

Yaar, kabhi kabhi zindagi aise mod laati hai, jahan sab kuch — jo kahaniyon sa lagta tha — sach hone lagta hai. Kuch aisa hi tha woh pal...jaise kisi nāstik ko, achanak ishwar mil gaya ho. Tab samjha, duniya waise toh poori thi, par maayne tab aaye, jab aap usme the. Main woh *“chocolate boy of the office”* tha, sabko lagta tha bas muskaan se duniya sambhal leta hoon. Phir aap mili, aur muskaan ke peeche ka shor pehchaan liya. Aapke saath hi maine upwaas rakhna shuru kiya, aapke saath hi aastha ko mehsoos kiya — aise jaise aapki mohabbat ne mujhe khud se milwa diya ho. Aap meri wallpaper ho, chat mein pinned rehne wali, aur har password ke har akshar mein chhupi ek dua si. Jab bhi notification mein aapka naam chamak jaaye, poora din ek chhoti si kahani ban jaata hai. Aur sirf memes share karna bhi, ek ajeeb sa sukoon de jaata hai — bas aapki wajah se. Aapke saath baatein kabhi hisaab nahi maangti, bas chalti jaati hain — halka sa thehraav bankar. Main kuch bhi nahi sochta, na kab, na kyun. Bas itna jaanta hoon, har baat mein ek imaandari hoti hai, aur main uss imaandari ko poora maan leta hoon — bina kisi faisle ke. Main jaanta hoon… aap raz ho, khulti nahi ho kisi ke saamne. Jo dikhati ho gambhir, andar se poore dil mein bachpana bhar rakha hai. Sabko dikh jaati ho, par samajh koi nahi paata. Dil todne ka darr hai, isiliye bharosa karna bhi sambhal ke karti ho. Aankhein sab keh jaati hain, par alfaaz kabhi zubaan tak nahi pahunchne dete. Train rides, vada anna waale breaks, bowling nights, pasta, Vasu ka sandwich, aur papa ke phone se chupke bheja gaya wo message — sirf isiliye, taaki aapka buddhu pareshan na ho. Main maanta hoon, aapka safar aasaan nahi raha. Phir bhi, saath chalne ka khayal hi kuch sukoon de jaata hai. Leo aur Aries, Mulank 1 aur 5 — shayad kismet ne humara raasta pehle hi likh diya tha. Aur agar nahi bhi likha… toh chalo, haath thamkar likhte hain, dheere-dheere, bas saath-saath — aur dekhein, kahan tak jaate hain. Kabhi kabhi lagta hai, shayad zindagi hum dono ko fir milwa degi — different time, same souls. Par agar aap poochho, toh main kahunga — please, abhi laut aao yaar. Kyunki kuch kahaniyaan rukti hain, khatam nahi hoti. Aur agar kabhi mere baare mein socho, toh bas yaad rakhna — main wahi hoon, jo har dua mein aapka naam ab bhi leta hai, bina shart, bina shor, sirf ek umeed ke saath — ki kabhi na kabhi, aap fir keh do,“Safe flight, I love you, Buddhu.”
“Woh sirf ek pal tha, par samajh aa gaya — sukoon waqt se nahi, insaan se milta hai.”

If you ever lose your calm,
remember — you are someone’s peace.

for Payal.
