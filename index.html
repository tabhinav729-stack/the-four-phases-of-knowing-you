The Five Phases of You
I’d once told her, “Rome wasn’t built in a day.”
She smiled — and quietly built it anyway.
Now, as she leaves, Rome stands ready —
made of calm, laughter, and light.
This space holds five poems —
a beginning, a becoming, a bond, a memory, and a moment that stayed.
Nothing to decode here — just moments that meant something, arranged gently in their own time.
for Payal.
